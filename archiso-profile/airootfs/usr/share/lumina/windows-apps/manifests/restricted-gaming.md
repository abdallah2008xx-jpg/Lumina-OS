# Restricted Gaming Workflow Manifest

## Linux-First Baseline

- Keep unsupported anti-cheat or restricted gaming workflows outside the official supported path.
- Use Lumina honestly: do not imply that unsupported titles are solved when they are not.

## Windows Workspace Baseline

- Do not build the official Lumina promise around stealth, hiding, or bypass behavior.
- Separate “can experiment privately” from “supported workflow” in every note and decision.

## First Session Checklist

- Confirm whether the title depends on kernel anti-cheat or platform restrictions.
- Keep it out of the supported workflow if it needs hidden VM behavior.
- Document it as out of scope instead of forcing a fragile promise.

## Risks

- The main risk is reputational: overpromising what Lumina does not officially support.
